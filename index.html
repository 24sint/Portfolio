About Me

Hello, My name is Sintayehu Mekonnen. I'm a Full-stack Developer. I love Coding, debugging, and Design!

From the beginning, I never had the chance to learn and use Computers when I was in my Hometown. However, learning about technology and using them was my dream. When I came to the USA in 2015, my first decision was to join the school. I attended Georgia Piedmont Technical College and received an Associate Degree last of 2018.

After a while, I met open, kind, and helpful volunteers at Friends of refugee became the spine to forward my vision. They taught us the basic skills of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ways of communications, building up networks, and more. My programming journey was started their and I built and presented my first website on the last day of our graduations in front of other guests.

Then, I continued teaching myself on online resources like Udemy is one of my favorite. I also joined other short-term boot camps to develop my skills. I am impressed when I see my work right now. I added more skills, started an internship, and getting more experience for my future goal. I want to thank Code the Dream team members for the internship opportunity to develop my knowledge and for this opportunity to disclose potential with the in-hand projects.
